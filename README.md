
# Marketing Analytics - RFM Customer Segmentation

A comprehensive analysis and segmentation of customers using **RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary)** methodology to enable targeted marketing strategies and customer lifecycle management.

## 📊 Project Overview

This project implements RFM analysis, a powerful data-driven approach to customer segmentation that evaluates customers based on three key metrics:

- **Recency (R)**: How recently a customer made a purchase
- **Frequency (F)**: How often a customer makes purchases
- **Monetary (M)**: How much money a customer has spent

## 🎯 Objectives

- Segment customers into meaningful groups based on their purchasing behavior
- Identify high-value customers for retention and upsell opportunities
- Recognize at-risk customers for re-engagement campaigns
- Enable data-driven marketing strategies and personalized outreach

## 📊 RFM Segmentation Output

The analysis produces customer segments such as:
- **Champions** - Best customers (high R, F, M)
- **Loyal Customers** - Repeat purchasers (high F, M)
- **At Risk** - Good customers but haven't purchased recently (low R)
- **Need Attention** - Medium-value customers (medium R, F, M)
- **Lost** - Haven't purchased in a long time (very low R)
